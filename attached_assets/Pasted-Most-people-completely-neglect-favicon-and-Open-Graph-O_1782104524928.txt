Most people completely neglect favicon and Open Graph (OG) cards.

For a website like this, they're disproportionately important because:

* Hospital CEOs will share it in WhatsApp
* Investors will paste it in email
* Board members will send LinkedIn messages
* Healthcare executives will open it from mobile

The OG card becomes the **first impression** before they even visit the site.

---

# Favicon Strategy

Do NOT use:

```text
MR
inside a blue square
```

That looks generic.

Instead:

### Option 1 (Recommended)

Use the existing logo style:

```text
White MR monogram
inside deep navy square
```

Colors:

```css
Background: #0F4C81
Letters: #FFFFFF
```

Minimal.

Executive.

Works at 16x16.

---

### Option 2 (Premium)

Create a custom monogram:

```text
M
R
```

interlocked like:

```text
M
└R
```

Inspired by:

* Harvard
* Goldman Sachs
* McKinsey partner profiles
