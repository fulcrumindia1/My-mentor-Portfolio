I agree with your instinct.

Looking at the final version, the site is already quite strong. 

The biggest remaining risk is **over-selling availability**.

---

# 1. "Available For" Section

Current version (Page 10):

```text
Available For

Board Advisory
Healthcare Transformation
Digital Health Strategy
Hospital Turnarounds
Healthcare Interoperability
Executive Mentorship
Speaking Engagements
Investment Advisory
```



For a consultant:

✅ Good

For a former CEO/CSO/COO:

⚠️ Starts looking like a service catalogue.

The more services you list, the less senior you appear.

---

## What Premium Executive Sites Do

Instead of:

```text
Available For
```

Use:

```text
Areas of Strategic Engagement
```

or

```text
Executive Advisory Focus
```

Then reduce to only:

```text
Board Advisory

Healthcare Transformation

Digital Health Strategy

Standards & Interoperability

Speaking Engagements
```

That's enough.

Remove:

❌ Executive Mentorship

❌ Investment Advisory

❌ Hospital Turnarounds

Those can still be mentioned elsewhere.
